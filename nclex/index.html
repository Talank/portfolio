Welcome to the NCLEX Mock Test

This mock test will simulate the NCLEX examination. The difficulty level of the questions will adjust based on your answers. You pass the exam if you answer 10 consecutive questions correctly at the highest difficulty level or if your overall score is above 70%. The exam begins when you click the "Start Test" button. Good luck!

The test itself is always free to use, signed in or not. Signing in just saves your score history across devices — otherwise it's remembered only in this browser.
